Timing
The effect of this Upgrade Card can be chosen during Attack Step 5: Resolve Damage

Important: The critical effect must be chosen and pronounced after the defender already spent his defense tokens (Attack Step 4). When the attacker decides for Dodonna's Pride effect, the Damage Total drops to zero, but the defender cannot undo his decision.

Appearance
The Dodonna's Pride was named for General Jan Dodonna. That CR90 Corvette was used by the Rebel Alliance in the real time strategy game Empire at War.
