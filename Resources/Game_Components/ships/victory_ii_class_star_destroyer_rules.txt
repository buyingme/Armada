Class Specifics
Compared to the Victory I-class Star Destroyer for 73 pts. the II-class variant is armed with 3 blue dice at the front instead of 3 black and at the side batteries with 1 blue die instead of 1 black. It has 1 slot for Ion Cannon Upgrade Cards instead of Ordnance Upgrade.

Appearance
The Victory-class Star Destroyer were the predecessor of the bigger and better armed Imperial-class Star Destroyer designed for the Galactic Republic but built by the Galactic Empire. The ship class was very popular in the D6 Pen&Paper RPG and appeared in several novels.
