Appearance
The TIE/IN interceptor, also known as TIE Interceptor, was created by Sienar Fleet Systems as the Empire's response to the Rebel Alliance's introduction of faster starships, and upon realization that the TIE Advanced x1 would be too costly to mass-produce. It first showd up in Episode VI most notably during the Battle of Endor. But it is also seen in the episode "The Antilles Extraction" of the TV-series Star Wars Rebels, which plays before the Battle of Scarif.
