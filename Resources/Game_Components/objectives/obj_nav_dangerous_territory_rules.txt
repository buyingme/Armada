Card Text
Setup: Obstacles must be placed in the setup area beyond distance 5 of both players' edges. After placing obstacles, place 1 objective token on each obstacle.

Special Rule: When a ship overlaps an obstacle, the ship's owner may remove the objective token on that obstacle to gain 1 victory token.

When one of the second player's ships overlaps an asteroid field or debris field, that obstacle has no effect.

Victory Token: 15 pt.

Rules Clarification
A squadron with strategic can move objective tokens off of or onto obstacles. An objective token can only be removed to gain 1 victory token if it is overlapping an obstacle. If multiple objective tokens are overlapping an obstacle, only 1 can be removed when a ship overlaps that obstacle.

FAQ 3.1.1 April 2017

Timing
This card’s Special Rule effect resolves during the Move Ship step while executing a maneuver.
