How-to play Objective "Precision Strike" - Star Wars Armada Explained (SWAE)
Instructions and hints by Karneck
Card Text
Setup: After deploying fleets, each of the second player's ships gains a Icon Command ConcentrateFire token.

Special Rule: When a squadron with Bomber or a ship is attacking, it may spend 1 die with a Icon Dice Hit icon to flip 1 random facedown damage card on the defender faceup.

After a squadron with Bomber or a ship performs an attack, its owner gains 1 victory token for each damage card that was dealt faceup or flipped faceup during that attack.

Victory Token: 15 pts.
