Appearance
The T-65B X-wing starfighter was used by the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. It became the backbone of the Rebel Alliance Starfighter Corps. They played a major part in all battles shown in the Original Trilogy. Newer X-wing models such as the T-70 X-wing fighter would eventually fall into the hands of the Resistance in Episode VII.
