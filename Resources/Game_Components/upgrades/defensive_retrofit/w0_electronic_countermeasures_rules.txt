New Rule
With Armada 1.5 published in December 2020 this card has been changed from Recur to non-Recur. You now have to pay 1 Icon Command Repair token during Status Phase to ready this card.

Card Text
"While defending you may exhaust this card to spend 1 defense token that your opponent targeted with an Icon Dice Accuracy result."

Rules Clarification
This card’s effect does not allow you to do any of the following:

Spend a defense token while at speed “0”.
Spend a defense token of a type the defender has already spent during an attack.
Spend a defense token more than once during an attack.
