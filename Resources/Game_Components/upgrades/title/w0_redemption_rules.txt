Timing
The effect of this Upgrade Card triggers during Ship Phase after step 1: "Reveal Command Dial", when resolving a Repair Command.

Appearance
The Redemption was an Nebulon-B Escort Frigate. She was refitted with advanced medical facilities and served the Rebel Alliance fleet as the primary medical frigate before and during the Battle of Endor. The ship appears at the end of Episode V and in Episode VI.
